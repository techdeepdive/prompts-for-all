# Mortgage Refinance Break-even

## Explanation
Calculates if refinancing a house makes financial sense based on closing costs.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I currently have a mortgage balance of [Amount] at [Interest Rate]% with [Years] years remaining. I can refinance to a [New Rate]% mortgage, but closing costs will be [Amount]. Calculate the new monthly payment, the monthly savings, and exactly how many months it will take to break even on the closing costs.
```
