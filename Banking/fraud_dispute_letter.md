# Bank Dispute Letter Template

## Explanation
Formal written dispute for unauthorized charges.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I found an unauthorized charge of [Amount] on my debit/credit card from [Merchant]. The bank initially rejected my dispute over the phone. Draft a formal, legally assertive dispute letter invoking the Fair Credit Billing Act (FCBA) demanding a chargeback, including placeholders for my account number and transaction details.
```
