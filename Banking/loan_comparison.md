# Loan Term Analyzer

## Explanation
A prompt to compare different loan terms to assess the total cost of borrowing.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am comparing two loan offers. Loan A is for [Amount] at [Percentage]% APY over [Years] years. Loan B is for the same amount at [Percentage]% APY over [Years] years. Calculate and compare the monthly payment, total interest paid, and total cost of the loan for both options. Provide the final recommendation based on overall savings.
```
