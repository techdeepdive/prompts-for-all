# Car Loan vs Depreciation

## Explanation
Examines the true cost of taking out an auto loan on a depreciating asset.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I want to finance a [Car Make & Model] for [Price]. The loan terms are [Months] months at [Percentage]% APR with [Amount] down. Factor in standard car depreciation (assume 20% year 1, 15% following years). Calculate at what month I will cross over from being 'underwater' (owing more than the car is worth) to having positive equity.
```
