# Credit Score Repair Steps

## Explanation
Actionable strategy for rapidly improving a FICO credit score.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as a credit counselor. My FICO score recently dropped to [Score] because of [Reason, e.g., high credit utilization and one late payment]. I want to buy a house in 12 months. Outline a rapid, month-by-month action plan to repair my credit score, including exactly when to pay my statements relative to the reporting date.
```
