# Credit Card Rewards Optimizer

## Explanation
Analyzes spending habits to recommend the best points/cashback strategy.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as a credit card rewards expert. My highest monthly spending categories are: [Category 1 - Amount], [Category 2 - Amount], and [Category 3 - Amount]. I prefer [Cashback / Travel Points]. Based on the current US market, recommend an optimal 2 or 3 credit card setup (the 'trifecta' approach) to maximize my rewards. Explain why each card is selected.
```
