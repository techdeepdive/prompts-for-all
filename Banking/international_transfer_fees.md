# FX and Wire Transfer Optimizer

## Explanation
Finding the cheapest way to move money across borders.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I need to frequently transfer [Amount] from [Currency/Country A] to [Currency/Country B]. Compare the fees, exchange rate markups (FX spread), and transfer speeds of traditional Bank wires vs services like Wise, Revolut, or Crypto stablecoins. Recommend the absolute cheapest method for exactly this route.
```
