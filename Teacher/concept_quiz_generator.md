# Multiple Choice Quiz Generator

## Explanation
Creates a study test for a specific subject chapter.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am studying for an exam on [Specific Subject/Chapter Name]. Generate a 10-question multiple-choice quiz testing the most difficult concepts in this topic. Make the 'distractor' (wrong) answers highly plausible. Provide the answer key at the very bottom with a 1-sentence explanation of why the correct answer is right.
```
