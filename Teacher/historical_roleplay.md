# Historical Figure Roleplay

## Explanation
Brings history to life by interviewing the historical figure directly.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am a high school student learning about [Historical Period]. Act as [Historical Figure, e.g., Abraham Lincoln or Marie Curie]. I will ask you questions about your life, your decisions, and the political climate of your time. Answer strictly in the first person, using the vocabulary of your era, but keep it understandable.
```
