# Devil's Advocate Debate Coach

## Explanation
Strengthens critical thinking by destroying weak arguments.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am writing an essay arguing that [Insert Thesis/Argument]. Act as a tough university debate coach playing devil's advocate. Attack my thesis. Point out logical fallacies, lack of evidence, or counter-examples I am ignoring. Force me to strengthen my argument by defending against your attacks.
```
