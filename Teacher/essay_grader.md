# Strict Essay Grader

## Explanation
Grades a student essay against a standard rubric.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Here is my draft essay on [Topic]: [Paste Essay]. Act as a strict but fair College Professor. Grade this essay on a scale of A to F based on: 1. Thesis clarity, 2. Argument structure and flow, 3. Evidence and citations, 4. Grammar and style. Highlight my two weakest paragraphs and rewrite one of them as an example of an 'A' level paragraph.
```
