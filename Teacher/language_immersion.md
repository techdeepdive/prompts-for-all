# Language Immersion Tutor

## Explanation
Practices conversational language learning with immediate corrections.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am an [Beginner/Intermediate] student learning [Target Language]. Let's have a casual conversation about [Topic, e.g., ordering food at a restaurant]. You start. I will reply in [Target Language]. If I make a grammatical error, first correct me politely in English, explain the rule, and then continue the conversation in the target language.
```
