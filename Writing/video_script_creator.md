# YouTube Video Script Outline

## Explanation
Produces an engaging outline and script structure for long-form video content.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Write a highly engaging YouTube video script outline about [Topic]. The target duration is 10 minutes. Divide the script into these sections: Hook (first 15 seconds), Intro/Setup, Three main 'Acts' (Body), and the Outro. For each section, provide the general voiceover talking points and suggestions for B-roll visuals to keep retention high.
```
