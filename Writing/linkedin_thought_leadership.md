# LinkedIn Thought Leadership Post

## Explanation
Creates a viral-styled LinkedIn post with line breaks and appropriate emojis.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as a top-tier LinkedIn ghostwriter. Write a thought-leadership post about a controversial opinion or deep insight regarding [Industry/Topic]. Structure it with a punchy hook (max 2 lines), a personal short story of a failure or realization, 3 bulleted takeaways, and a question at the end to drive comments. Use plenty of whitespace.
```
