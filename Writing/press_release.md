# Standard Press Release

## Explanation
Generates a professional PR document for news distribution.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Write a formal Press Release announcing the [Launch/Acquisition/Event] of [Company/Product Name]. Include a strong headline, dateline, an introductory paragraph answering the 5 Ws (Who, What, When, Where, Why), a quote from the CEO [CEO Name], a body paragraph detailing the significance, and an 'About the Company' boilerplate section at the bottom.
```
