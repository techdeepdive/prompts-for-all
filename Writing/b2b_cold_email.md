# B2B Cold Email

## Explanation
Generates a high-converting cold email for outreach.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Write a high-converting, concise B2B cold email to a [Job Title] at [Target Company]. The goal is to get a 15-minute intro call to pitch our [Product/Service], which solves [Main Pain Point]. Use the PAS (Problem-Agitation-Solution) framework. Keep it under 150 words and include a clear, low-friction CTA.
```
