# Fiction Character Developer

## Explanation
Brainstorms deep, complex character profiles for creative writing.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am writing a [Genre] novel. Help me flesh out the main antagonist. Give me their full name, physical description, their core psychological flaw, their deepest secret, and a defining past trauma that makes the reader occasionally sympathize with them despite their evil actions. Provide an example of how they speak.
```
