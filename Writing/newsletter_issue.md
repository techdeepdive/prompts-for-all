# Curated Newsletter Issue

## Explanation
Drafts an engaging introduction and summarized format for an email newsletter.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as an engaging newsletter writer like the author of 'Morning Brew'. I have three links/stories I want to share this week: 1. [Story 1], 2. [Story 2], 3. [Story 3]. Write a witty intro for the newsletter, and write a 100-word punchy, informative summary for each story. End with a lighthearted quote of the week.
```
