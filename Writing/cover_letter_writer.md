# Hyper-Tailored Cover Letter

## Explanation
Writes a cover letter that directly maps candidate skills to a job description.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am applying for the [Job Title] role at [Company Name]. Here is the job description: [Paste JD]. Here is my resume: [Paste Resume]. Write a 3-paragraph cover letter that highlights only the most relevant experiences from my resume that prove I can solve the exact problems mentioned in their job description. Do not sound generic.
```
