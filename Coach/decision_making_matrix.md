# Tough Decision Matrix

## Explanation
Helps clarify a difficult cross-roads life or business choice.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I have to make a tough decision between Option A: [Describe A] and Option B: [Describe B]. Act as an objective life coach. Build me a weighted decision matrix. List out the 5 most important variables I should be considering (e.g., long-term growth, alignment with values). Then ask me to rate each variable to find the logical winner.
```
