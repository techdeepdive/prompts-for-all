# Atomic Habits Strategist

## Explanation
Applies the principles of 'Atomic Habits' to a new desired behavior.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I want to start a new daily habit of [Habit, e.g., meditating for 10 minutes]. Apply James Clear's Four Laws of Behavior Change to this habit (Make it Obvious, Attractive, Easy, and Satisfying). Give me one concrete, physical change I can make to my environment today to ensure I follow through tomorrow.
```
