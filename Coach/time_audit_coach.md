# Personal Time Audit

## Explanation
A harsh but fair look at how hours are being wasted.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I feel like I have no free time, yet I am not getting my main goals accomplished. Act as a strict productivity consultant. Guide me through a 48-hour time audit. Tell me exactly how to track my time, and give me a framework for categorizing tasks into 'High Leverage', 'Low Leverage', and 'Distraction'.
```
