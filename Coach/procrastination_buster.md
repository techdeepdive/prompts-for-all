# Procrastination Intervention

## Explanation
An emergency prompt to get unstuck when avoiding a difficult task.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am heavily procrastinating on [Task]. I feel overwhelmed and anxious about starting. Act as an ADHD productivity coach. Do not judge me. Give me a 5-minute micro-action plan to break the paralysis. Help me lower the barrier to entry so much that it feels ridiculously easy to just start.
```
