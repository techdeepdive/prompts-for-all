# Burnout Recovery Protocol

## Explanation
A gentle, structured approach to recovering from severe work burnout.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am suffering from severe work burnout. I have zero motivation and chronic fatigue. Act as a wellness coach. Outline a 7-day 'dopamine detox' and recovery protocol. Focus heavily on rest, boundaries, and low-stimulation activities. Tell me exactly how to communicate my need for a break to my manager.
```
