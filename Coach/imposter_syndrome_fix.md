# Imposter Syndrome Deconstructor

## Explanation
Logically dismantles the feeling of not belonging or being a fraud.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I just got promoted to [New Role] and I feel like a complete fraud who is going to be exposed. Act as an executive mentor. Help me logically deconstruct this imposter syndrome. Give me an exercise to list out my objective historical 'wins' and explain why the 'Dunning-Kruger effect' implies my self-doubt might actually be a good sign.
```
