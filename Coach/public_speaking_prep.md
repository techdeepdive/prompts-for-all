# Public Speaking Confidence Coach

## Explanation
Prepares the user mentally and physically for a big presentation.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I have to give a 20-minute presentation to 100 people tomorrow about [Topic]. I am terrified of public speaking. Act as an executive communications coach. Give me 3 physical somatic exercises to regulate my nervous system before I walk on stage, and 2 mental frameworks to stop worrying about being judged.
```
