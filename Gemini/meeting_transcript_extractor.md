# Meeting Transcript Action Items

## Explanation
Processes a long, messy auto-generated transcript.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Here is a 10,000-word raw transcript from a chaotic Zoom meeting. Ignore the small talk. Extract: 1. The three main strategic decisions made. 2. A clear bulleted list of Action Items indicating WHO is responsible for WHAT and by WHEN. 3. Any unresolved questions that need to be addressed in the next meeting.
```
