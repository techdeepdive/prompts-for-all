# Video Scene/Frame Analyzer

## Explanation
Utilizes Gemini 1.5 Pro's long-context video understanding capabilities.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am attaching a [Number]-minute video. Watch the entire video. First, provide a chronological index with timestamps of the 5 most significant scene changes. Second, answer this specific question based only on the visual information in the video: [Ask specific visual question, e.g., 'What color was the car that drove past at minute 2?'].
```
