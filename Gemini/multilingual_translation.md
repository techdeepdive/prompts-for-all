# Nuanced Multilingual Translation

## Explanation
Uses Gemini's superior native language generation to translate idioms, not just literal words.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Translate the following English paragraph into [Target Language]. Do not give a rigid, literal translation. Ensure that you adapt English idioms into equivalent cultural idioms in [Target Language]. The tone should be conversational and friendly. Include a reverse translation and explanation for any slang you used.
```
