# Financial Chart Interpreter

## Explanation
Upload a screenshot of a trading chart and have Gemini interpret the technicals.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Attached is a screenshot of a daily candlestick chart for [Asset]. Identify the overarching trend. Point out any clear support and resistance levels. Do you see any classic chart patterns formed (like head and shoulders or bull flags)? Finally, explain what the RSI and MACD indicators (if visible) are suggesting.
```
