# Habit Tracker Progress Bar

## Explanation
Provides the Notion formula to calculate daily habit completion and display a progress bar.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I have a Notion database for habit tracking with 5 checkbox properties: Habit 1, Habit 2, Habit 3, Habit 4, Habit 5. Generate a Notion formula that calculates the percentage of completed habits for the day and outputs a visual progress bar (using emojis or characters like 🟩 and ⬜) along with the percentage text.
```
