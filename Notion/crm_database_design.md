# Sales CRM Builder

## Explanation
Designs a relational database tracking clients, interactions, and potential deal revenue.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as a sales operations manager. How do I build a lightweight CRM entirely in Notion? Tell me the exact properties needed for a 'Companies' database and a 'Contacts/Leads' database, how to relate them, and how to create a rollup property to calculate the total estimated Deal Value per Company.
```
