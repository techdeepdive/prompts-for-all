# Academic/Non-Profit Grant Writer

## Explanation
Produces formal, highly detailed persuasive writing for funding applications.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as an experienced grant writer. I need to write a proposal for a [Amount] grant from [Funding Organization] to fund our project which does [Project Description]. Draft the 'Needs Statement', 'Goals and Objectives', 'Methodology', and 'Evaluation Plan' sections. Ensure the tone is highly academic, persuasive, and data-driven.
```
