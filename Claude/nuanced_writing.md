# Nuanced Creative Writing

## Explanation
Utilizes Claude's highly natural, less 'AI-sounding' writing style.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Write a short story about [Premise]. Focus on deep, psychological character development and 'show, don't tell' descriptive prose. The tone should be melancholic but hopeful. Avoid using cliches and standard AI-isms like 'tapestry' or 'in conclusion'. Make the dialogue sound highly realistic and slightly awkward.
```
