# Legal Contract Analyzer

## Explanation
Uses Claude's nuanced comprehension to find loopholes in legal jargon.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as an expert corporate lawyer. I am pasting the text of a [Type of Contract, e.g., Non-Disclosure Agreement or Freelance Contract]. Read it carefully. Highlight any clauses that are overly broad or disproportionately favor the other party. Suggest specific, red-lined modifications to language to make the contract more mutually fair.
```
