# Anti-Bias Political Summarizer

## Explanation
Tries to leverage Grok to get a non-sanitized view of a political event.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
A major political event just happened: [Describe Event]. Give me a completely unvarnished, anti-establishment breakdown. Tell me exactly how the left-wing media on X is spinning this, and exactly how the right-wing media on X is spinning this. Point out the hypocrisy in both arguments.
```
