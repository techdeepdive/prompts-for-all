# Crypto Twitter Sentiment Analyst

## Explanation
Utilizes Grok's access to Crypto-Twitter to gauge market hype.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Scan X for recent mentions of [Cryptocurrency Ticker/Name]. Are influential accounts bullish or bearish right now? Are there rumors of upcoming announcements, airdrops, or mainnet launches? Aggregate this data and give me a 'Hype Score' from 1-10 with a witty breakdown.
```
