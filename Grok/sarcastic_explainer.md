# Sarcastic Tech Explainer

## Explanation
Uses Grok's 'fun mode' to explain complex topics humorously.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Explain the concept of [Complex Topic, e.g., Quantum Entanglement or the Federal Reserve] as if I am a 5-year-old, but you are extremely sarcastic and slightly annoyed that you have to explain this to a human. Keep it factually accurate despite the snarky tone.
```
