# Ruthless Startup Idea Roast

## Explanation
Grok's unrestrained nature is perfect for finding the brutal flaws in an idea.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Here is my new startup idea: [Describe Idea]. I want you to completely destroy this idea. Roast it ruthlessly. What are the gaping holes in my business model? Why will no one buy this? Why will established competitors crush me? Do not hold back, be as brutally honest and cynical as possible.
```
