# Tech Founder Drama Timeline

## Explanation
Assembles a timeline of recent founder beefs in Silicon Valley.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
There seems to be recent drama on X between [Tech Founder A] and [Tech Founder B]. Assemble a chronological timeline of the key tweets, passive-aggressive replies, and sub-tweets that summarize this beef over the last 72 hours. Add your own sarcastic commentary on who is 'winning'.
```
