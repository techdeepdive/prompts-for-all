# Plateau Breaker Protocol

## Explanation
Strategies for when the scale or the barbell stops moving.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I have hit a strict plateau. I have been stuck at [Current Weight / Current Lift Name & Weight] for 4 weeks despite being consistent. Act as an expert coach. Give me a checklist of the top 3 physiological variables (like diet fatigue, sleep, or volume) that I need to audit, and prescribe an intervention (e.g., diet break, deload, change mechanics) to break through.
```
