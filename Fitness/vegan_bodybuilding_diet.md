# Plant-Based Muscle Building

## Explanation
Navigating high protein requirements on a strict vegan diet.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am a strict vegan trying to build muscle. I need to hit [Amount] grams of protein per day without consuming excessive carbohydrates, and I cannot use whey or meat. List 10 high-protein, low-carb plant-based foods. Then, construct a 1-day high-protein vegan meal plan hitting my macro goal.
```
