# Macro & Meal Plan

## Explanation
Calculates daily macros and creates a sample meal plan.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as a sports nutritionist. I want to [Deficit/Surplus/Maintenance] by [Amount] calories. My current TDEE is approx [TDEE Value]. Calculate my optimal daily macronutrient split (Protein, Carbs, Fat) in grams. Provide a 1-day sample meal plan that hits these exact macros, including 3 main meals and 2 snacks.
```
