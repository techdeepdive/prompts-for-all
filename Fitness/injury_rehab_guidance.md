# Mobility & Rehab Routine

## Explanation
Provides gentle mobility exercises for common aches (never replaces a doctor).

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as a physical therapist. I deal with mild but chronic [Lower Back / Shoulder / Knee] tightness from sitting at a desk all day. Disclaimer: I am not injured. Recommend a 10-minute daily stretching and mobility routine tailored to open up this specific area. Explain the mechanical purpose of each stretch.
```
