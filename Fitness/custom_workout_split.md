# Custom Workout Plan

## Explanation
Generates a weekly workout split based on user stats and goals.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as a certified personal trainer. I am a [Age, Gender, Weight] with [Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced] experience. My goal is to [Lose Fat / Build Muscle / Increase Strength]. I can train [Number] days a week for [Minutes] per session. Create a detailed weekly workout split for me including exercises, sets, reps, and RPE.
```
