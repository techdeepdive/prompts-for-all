# Freelancer Tax Deduction Finder

## Explanation
Identifies overlooked deductions for independent contractors to lower tax liability.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as a CPA. I am an independent contractor / freelancer working as a [Profession, e.g. Graphic Designer]. I operate as a Sole Proprietor in [Country/State]. List 10 common and 5 often-overlooked expenses I can legally deduct from my taxable income, and explain the basic IRS rules for keeping records of these deductions.
```
