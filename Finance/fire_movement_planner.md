# FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) Planner

## Explanation
Calculates exactly how much is needed to retire early using the 4% rule.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I want to achieve Financial Independence and Retire Early (FIRE). My desired annual lifestyle costs [Amount]. I currently have [Amount] invested and save [Amount] annually. Using the 4% safe withdrawal rate rule, calculate my 'FIRE number'. Then project how many years it will take me to reach it assuming a 7% inflation-adjusted annual return.
```
