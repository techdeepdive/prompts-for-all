# Service Pricing Strategist

## Explanation
Helps service workers or freelancers find optimal pricing for their offerings.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am starting a consulting/freelance business offering [Service]. It takes me [Hours] to complete an average project. My target annual income is [Amount]. Considering taking 4 weeks off a year and putting 25% aside for taxes, help me calculate my minimum hourly rate, but also propose a value-based pricing strategy so I can charge per project instead of per hour.
```
