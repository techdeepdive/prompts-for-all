# ETF Investment Strategy Formulation

## Explanation
Generates a diversified ETF portfolio based on risk tolerance and age.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am [Age] years old with a [Low/Medium/High] risk tolerance. I want to invest [Initial Amount] and contribute [Monthly Amount] regularly. Design a globally diversified ETF portfolio allocation strategy, explaining the rationale behind each asset class percentage. Note: Do not provide financial advice, just educational strategy.
```
