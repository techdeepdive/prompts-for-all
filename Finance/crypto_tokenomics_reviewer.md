# Crypto Tokenomics Reviewer

## Explanation
Evaluates the supply/demand dynamics of a new crypto token.

```text

A new project has the following token structure: Total supply [Amount], Initial circulating supply [Amount], team vesting schedule [Describe Schedule], and utility [Describe Utility].
