# Personal Budget Planner

## Explanation
A prompt for generating a structured monthly budget plan based on an individual's income and expenses.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as a certified financial planner. My monthly take-home income is [Amount]. My fixed expenses total [Amount] and variable expenses total [Amount]. Create a detailed 50/30/20 budget breakdown. Also, provide 3 actionable strategies on how I can increase my savings rate without drastically cutting down on my lifestyle.
```
