# Startup Runway Modeler

## Explanation
Builds a basic cash flow and runway model for early-stage founders.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am the founder of a startup. Our current cash balance is [Amount]. Our Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) is [Amount] and growing at [Percentage]% month-over-month. Our monthly burn rate (expenses) is [Amount]. Create a simple 12-month cash flow projection and calculate our exact cash-out date (runway). Suggest two ways to extend the runway by 3 months.
```
