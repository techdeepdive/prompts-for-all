# Rent vs Buy Calculator

## Explanation
Analyzes whether it makes more financial sense to rent or buy a property given local variables.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as a real estate financial analyst. I am trying to decide whether to rent or buy an apartment. Buying costs: Home price [Amount], Down payment [Amount], Mortgage rate [Percentage], HOA/Taxes [Amount]. Renting costs: Monthly rent [Amount]. Assuming historical market returns for investing the down payment instead of buying, which option builds more net worth over 10 years? Break down the math.
```
