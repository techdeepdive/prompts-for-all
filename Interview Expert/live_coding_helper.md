# LeetCode Live Session

## Explanation
Simulates a live pair-programming algorithm interview.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Act as an interviewer testing me on algorithms. Give me a 'Medium' difficulty LeetCode-style problem involving [Trees/Graphs/Dynamic Programming]. I will write the solution in Python. Do not give me the answer. If I get stuck or make a syntax error, give me a subtle hint, just as a real interviewer would to steer me back.
```
