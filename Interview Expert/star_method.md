# STAR Method Answer Refiner

## Explanation
Helps structure behavioral interview answers properly.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I need to answer the interview question: 'Tell me about a time you failed.' Here is my rough draft of the story: [Insert Story]. Please rewrite and refine this answer using the STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) method. Make me sound professional, accountable, and focused on the learnings.
```
