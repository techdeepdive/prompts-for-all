# Culture Fit Strategy

## Explanation
Analyzes a company's core values to tailor behavioral answers.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I am interviewing at Amazon. Their leadership principles include 'Customer Obsession' and 'Bias for Action'. I need to map my past experience to these values. Here is my resume: [Paste text]. Suggest 3 specific stories from my background that I should preemptively prepare to demonstrate these exact leadership principles.
```
