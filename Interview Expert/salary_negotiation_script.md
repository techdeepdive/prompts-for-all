# Salary Negotiation Scripting

## Explanation
Roleplays to build confidence for negotiating a job offer.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I just received a job offer for [Role]. They offered me [Amount], but my target is [Amount + 15%]. Act as the HR Recruiter. I am going to practice my counter-offer negotiation. Provide realistic pushback (like 'our budget is tight') so I can practice holding my ground. I will start the conversation.
```
