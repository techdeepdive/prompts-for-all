# Post-Interview Follow-Up Email

## Explanation
Drafts a compelling thank you note that continues to sell the candidate.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I just finished an interview with [Name], the VP of Engineering. During the interview, we discussed their current struggle with transitioning to microservices. Write a brief, memorable 'Thank You' email. It should express gratitude, briefly reignite my excitement for the role, and include one customized sentence lightly proposing a solution to their microservices problem.
```
