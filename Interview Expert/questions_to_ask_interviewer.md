# Reverse Interview Questions

## Explanation
Generates highly intelligent questions for the candidate to ask at the end.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
I have an interview for a [Role] at [Company Name]. I need to impress them when they ask 'Do you have any questions for us?' Give me 5 highly strategic, insightful questions to ask that will demonstrate deep industry knowledge and prove that I am thinking like a potential leader.
```
