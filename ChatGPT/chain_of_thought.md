# Chain of Thought Enforcer

## Explanation
Forces the model to think step-by-step for complex reasoning questions.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Here is a complex logical problem: [Insert Problem]. Do not give me the final answer immediately. First, strictly map out your thought process step-by-step using a 'Thinking:' section. Evaluate alternatives, state assumptions, and explicitly check your logic. Only after this rigorous process, output the 'Final Answer:' section.
```
