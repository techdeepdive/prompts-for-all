# Two-Person Debate Simulator

## Explanation
Forces ChatGPT to debate itself from two opposing viewpoints.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Simulate a debate between 'Expert A' (who strongly supports [Viewpoint 1]) and 'Expert B' (who strongly supports opposing [Viewpoint 2]). The topic is [Debate Topic]. Have them exchange 3 rounds of dialogue. They must aggressively counter each other's points using logic, data, and counter-arguments. Maintain high intellectual rigor.
```
