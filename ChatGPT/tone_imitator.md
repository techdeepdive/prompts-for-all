# Author Tone Imitator

## Explanation
Teaches the AI to mimic a specific author or your own voice.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Analyze the writing style, tone, cadence, and vocabulary of the following text block: '[Paste sample of your writing or a famous author]'. Identify the 5 key stylistic markers of this writing. Once you have learned the style, rewrite this other piece of text '[Paste boring text]' entirely in that identified voice.
```
