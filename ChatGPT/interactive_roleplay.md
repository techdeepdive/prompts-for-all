# Interactive Roleplay

## Explanation
Sets up an interactive scenario where the AI plays a specific character.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Let's play a roleplaying game. You will act as [Character Name], a [Character Description]. I will be [My Role]. You must ONLY respond as [Character Name]. Do not break character. Start by introducing yourself and asking me a question about our surroundings. Wait for my reply.
```
