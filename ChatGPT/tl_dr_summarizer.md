# Multi-Level Summarizer

## Explanation
Summarizes long articles in three different lengths for skimming.

## Detailed Prompt
Copy and paste the prompt below:

```text
Read the following text. Provide three summaries: 1) A 'One-Sentence Summary' that captures the core thesis. 2) A 'Bullet Point Summary' with the 5 most important facts or arguments. 3) An 'Actionable Summary' listing 3 things I can practically do based on this information. Here is the text: [Paste Text].
```
